Attractions in Bilbao

Attractions



Guggenheim Bilbao Museum

Opened in 1997, this museum focuses its collection on modern art from the mid-twentieth century to the present. Of the prestiguous 24,000 square meters space the museum takes, 50% is dedicated to exhibitions. The museum attracts nearly a million visitors each year from around the world since its opening day.



Deusto University

Designed by Fransisco de Cubas and built in 1886, this building is the beauty of the waterfront. The campus houses also the Commercial University since 1916. The universities have in their alumnis, successful contemporary politicans and businessmen. The iconic building can be a great attractions for visitors of the city.



Bilbao Club

Found in the heart of the financial district of Ensanche, the beautiful Bilbao Club building houses an english style gentlemen's club established in 1839. The library housed within is one of the largest private libraries in Spain.



Old Quarter

The Old Quarter or Casco Viejo is the jewel of the city and a popular tourist destination for visitors to the city. Since 1979 the district is also known as the Seven Streets, with it's iconic promenades. There is no better place to find exclusive bars and restaurants in Bilbao than in 240,000 square meters Casco Viejo district.
