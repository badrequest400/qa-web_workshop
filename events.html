Events in Bilbao

Events



El Sol Festival - 28/05/2015-30/05/2015

A prestigous Latin American advertising communication event. The festival attracts thousands of professionals from all over the world, to network and share ideas. The festival welcomes all visitors with open doors to take part in the event.



Bilbao Triathlon - 30/05/2015-30/05/2015

A triathlon that takes the participants along a route of sightseeing in the city. The route is designed to showcase the msot iconic buildings and attractions of the city. The competition consists of a 1,900-metre swim along the river, a 90-mile bike race in two laps and a 21-kilometre running race in three laps.



Fant Fantastic Film Festival - 08/05/2015-15/05/2015

Organised by the city council's Department of Culture and Education, this film festival showcases competiton entries by local talent, but screens films by reputable international directors as well. It proves to be a treat for any visitors to the city every year.
